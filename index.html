About me

Hi there! My name is Finnley and I am currently a student at McGill University studying computer science. I have always been fascinated by the power of technology to solve problems and make a difference in the world. This led me to pursue a career in software engineering where I can apply my knowledge and skills to create innovative and impactful solutions.

My main interests lie in web development, game development, and machine learning. I enjoy the creative process of designing and building interactive and user-friendly websites, and I am excited about the potential of games to be not just entertainment but a powerful medium for education and social change. I am also fascinated by the capabilities of machine learning and artificial intelligence, and I am eager to learn more about its applications.

Projects

Bridge

Bridge is a website specifically designed to help couples in long distance relationships stay connected. The website offers various features such as a chat room, photo gallery, activity planner, and a timer that counts down to the next in-person meeting. These tools allow couples to communicate and share special moments together, even when they are miles apart. The website was developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Django, providing a user-friendly interface. Bridge serves as a platform for couples to strengthen their relationship and feel close despite the distance.

All Rat Remains

All Rat Remains is a horror puzzle game where players take on the role of a rat and uncover the mysterious history of an abandoned research lab. The game features a variety of mazes and escape rooms to challenge players as they unravel the lab's past with a surprise twist at the end.

The game received recognition for its audio design, winning the Best Audio Design award presented by Ubisoft at the MCGameJam 2023. The game's unique perspective as a rat, the challenging mazes and escape rooms, and the horror elements, creates an immersive and tension-filled experience. The surprise twist at the end adds an extra layer of intrigue, making it an unforgettable gaming experience. The use of Unity and C# ensures that the game runs smoothly and provides a seamless experience for players.
